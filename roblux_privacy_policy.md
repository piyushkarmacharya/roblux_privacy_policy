When you use the app, we may automatically collect limited technical information, such as:

- Device type and operating system
- App version

The app uses third-party services, including:

- **Google Firebase Analytics**

These services may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies.

### c) Web Content
The app may display web content through an in-app browser (WebView).  
When you access third-party web pages, those sites may collect data under their own policies.

We use collected information to:

- Operate and maintain the app
